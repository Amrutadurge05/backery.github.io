    ....About Our Bakery....

    Why Rain ?

    Rain's Bakery opened in may 2021, after an 18 month refit to our wee shop right in the heart of Fort William, on Cameron Square. We knew it was a good spot, because we've been running our main business, The Granite House, on the High Street since 1977.
    We knew that we needed a memorable name for the new business, so we opted for one of the things Fort William is already famous for rain..........

    About Us

    Welcome to our Rain's Bakery!
    We are passionate about creating delicious and visually stunning cakes that make every occasion special. Our journey began may 2021, and since then, we have been dedicated to crafting high-quality treats for our customers.

    At Rain's Bakery, we believe in using only the finest ingredients to ensure that every bite is a delightful experience. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or any other milestone, our talented team is here to bring your vision to life.

    Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to exceed expectations with every order. From classic flavors to custom designs, we're committed to providing you with cakes that not only taste amazing but also leave a lasting impression.

    Thank you for choosing Rain's Bakery for your sweet celebrations. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your memorable moments !
